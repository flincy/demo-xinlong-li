Xinlong Li

I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at Nanyang Business School, Nanyang Technological University.

My research centers on developing quantitative methods that leverage structural modeling and state-of-the-art machine learning techniques to gain a deeper understanding of customer behavior and guide firms' decisions.
